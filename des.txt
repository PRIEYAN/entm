=====================================================
  OFFLINE VOICE TRANSLATOR - COST & PERFORMANCE
=====================================================
  English -> Hindi speech translation. Fully offline.
  Runs on a Raspberry Pi. No internet, no cloud.
  (Prices approximate, INR.)
=====================================================


LATENCY (the headline)
----------------------
  RIGHT NOW  (Raspberry Pi 3 B+, 1 GB RAM):   35 - 40 seconds

  AFTER UPGRADE:
     Raspberry Pi 4 (4 GB RAM):   ~10 - 15 seconds   (about 3x faster)
     Raspberry Pi 5 (4-8 GB):     ~3 - 6 seconds     (about 6-8x faster)

  Why: the work is CPU-bound. A faster board = faster translation.
  The model already fits in RAM and is fully optimized (int8, greedy).
  (Pi 4/5 numbers are estimates from CPU benchmarks, not yet measured.)

A REAL-TIME, ALL-IN-ONE DEVICE (future)
---------------------------------------
  A single handheld unit doing everything on-device (mic + speech
  recognition + translation + speaker), near real-time (1-3 sec):

     Pi 5 (8 GB) + AI accelerator + mic + speaker
     + battery + screen + case:
                          ~22,000 - 33,000 INR   (~$260 - $400)
                          latency: ~1 - 3 seconds

  For comparison: shop-bought translator devices cost ~15,000-30,000 INR
  and most still NEED the internet. Ours works fully offline.


THE POINT
---------
  We proved a real AI translation model can run offline on a
  ~5,000-rupee, 1 GB device. Making it faster is just a hardware
  upgrade - the software and model stay exactly the same.

  Advantages: works with NO internet, voice never leaves the device
  (private), and very low cost.
